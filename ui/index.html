Halcyon Credit
Agentic Underwriting Copilot

Careful underwriting.
At machine speed.

Halcyon Credit's underwriting copilot is a multi-agent AI system that reviews a loan application the way a senior underwriter would — it independently verifies income, examines credit history, and applies lending policy, then scores repayment risk with a machine-learning model trained on 1.3 million real loans. A language model drafts the final recommendation with written, evidence-cited reasons, and an automated judge verifies that every claim is grounded in the applicant's actual data before the decision is recorded to a permanent audit trail. No black boxes — every decision is explainable, defensible, and fully traceable.

01

Verify

Income and credit-bureau data are independently confirmed by dedicated verification agents before anything is decided.

02

Assess

A gradient-boosted risk model scores repayment probability, with feature-level attribution explaining what drove the score.

03

Comply

Every application is checked against Halcyon's lending policy — hard-stop rules are enforced deterministically, never by the AI alone.

04

Audit

Decisions ship with written reasons, a faithfulness evaluation, and a step-by-step trace a regulator could review.
